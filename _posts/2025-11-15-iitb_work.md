<h4> Motivation for the Research </h4>




<ul class="clean-list">
  <li>The global push towards carbon neutrality and sustainable development has placed nations under immense pressure to reduce their carbon footprint.</li>
  <li>High-pressure storage vessels have emerged as a leading solution alongside traditional batteries to address these challenges.</li>
  <li>US and EU Fuel Cell Technologies Mission aims to reduce cost of hydrogen to 1$ per 1 kg in 1 decade</li>
    <li>Indian National Hydrogen Mission plans to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 45 % by 2030</li>

</ul>


<h4> Development of Composite Overwrapped Pressure Vessel </h4>

A TYPE-IV cylinder typically has a polymeric inner liner with a metallic boss to facilitate the flow of gas. In order to sustain high pressures it is strengthened with fiber reinforced polymer composite using wet or tow preg filament winding method.
